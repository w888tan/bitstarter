This site provides both organic suppliers and buyers to source, communicate and identify opportunities; at the same time, allowing the community to contribute to sustainable farm businesses. Some suppliers find it difficult to break into the market. For buyers, they might want to find products that are not grown locally. For suppliers, like farmers, they can ask what is needed for them to create a niche market or offer their unique products. By communication, farmers can plan and not overproduce, saving them the effort of finding another buyer for the extra supply. For the customer, it will be finding quality and unique products and offering long-term food and farm future.

How does this work for suppliers:

  • Create profile for your farm
  • Tag product lines

  • Search for product and contact suppliers
  • Create a business relationship
